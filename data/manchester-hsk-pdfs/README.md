# data/manchester-hsk-pdfs/

HSK 2.0 vocabulary list PDFs from the University of Manchester Confucius Institute.

**Source:** https://www.confuciusinstitute.manchester.ac.uk/study/testing/hsk/hsk-learning-resources/
**License:** University publication — use for reference only, do not redistribute

| File | Words | Format |
|------|-------|--------|
| hsk-level-1.pdf | ~150 | Chinese words in columns (no translations) |
| hsk-level-2.pdf | ~150 | Chinese words in columns (no translations) |
| hsk-level-3.pdf | ~300 | Chinese words in columns (no translations) |
| hsk-level-4.pdf | ~600 | Chinese words in columns (no translations) |
| hsk-level-5.pdf | ~1300 | Chinese words in columns (no translations) |
| hsk-level-6.pdf | ~2500 | Numbered table: No. / Chinese / Pinyin / English |

Note: levels 1–5 contain only Chinese words with no additional data.
Level 6 is a different format with a full numbered table including Pinyin and English.

Extracted word lists are in `data/manchester-hsk-vocab.json`.
Use `scripts/extract-manchester-hsk.py` to re-extract (requires `pdfminer.six` or `pdftotext`).
